Surah Al Fatihah


    Makki Surah


    بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ


    With the name of Allah, the extremely loving and caring right now, the always loving and caring.


    ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ


    1. All praise and thanks is for Allah, Master of all nations and people.


    ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ


    2. The extremely loving and caring right now, the always loving and caring.


    مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ


    3. The owner and king of the day of judgment.


    إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ


    4. You alone do we enslaves ourselves to, and You alone do we seek help from.


    ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ


    5. Guide us upon the straight path, which is upwards.


    صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ


    6. The path of those whom You have favored.


    غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ


    7. But not the path of those who had wrath upon them, nor of the lost.
